#  Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer data to understand churn behavior and retention patterns. The goal is to identify why customers leave, which segments are at risk, and how businesses can improve customer retention.

This analysis simulates real-world scenarios in SaaS and subscription-based businesses where reducing churn directly impacts revenue growth.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze customer churn rate and retention trends  
- Identify high-risk customer segments  
- Understand customer lifetime patterns  
- Discover key factors influencing churn  
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve retention  

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard & visualization)  
- Excel / CSV (Dataset handling)  

---

##  Key KPIs
- Total Customers  
- Churn Rate (%)  
- Average Tenure  
- Active vs Churned Customers  

---

## Dashboard Features
- **Churn Distribution** – Visual representation of churn vs retained customers  
- **Churn by Contract Type** – Identifies high-risk contract segments  
- **Tenure Analysis** – Shows how long customers stay before churning  
- **Monthly Charges vs Churn** – Analyzes pricing impact on churn  
- **Service Usage Analysis** – Examines how features affect retention  

---

##  Key Insights
- Customers with **month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn rate  
- Customers with **low tenure (<12 months)** are more likely to churn  
- Higher **monthly charges** are associated with increased churn probability  
- Customers without **tech support or online security** tend to churn more  
- Long-term contract customers show better retention  

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Encourage users to shift from **monthly to long-term contracts**  
- Improve onboarding experience for **new customers**  
- Offer **loyalty benefits or discounts** for long-term users  
- Provide better **customer support and service features**  
- Optimize pricing strategies to reduce churn  

---
